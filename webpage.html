Barrett The Service Dog

Everything you ever wanted to know about him, written by his partner, Lucy Beth

General Info about Barrett

Birthday: July 8, 2016

Breed: English Black Labrador

Type of Service Dog: Mobility

Gentle Leader: Barrett wears a red strap over his nose called a gentle leader. This is not a muzzle and he can open his mouth fully. It is used to prevent Barrett from pulling on his leash by cutting his power in half as he pulls on his own nose. This does not hurt him.

Abilities: Open and close doors using tug rope, push buttons, turn light switches on and off, open the fridge and take something out then close it and bring item, fetch objects, find a scented phone, tell a secret, give kisses, obedience commands.

Grooming: Every three months Barrett gets a full bath. His coat is brushed everyday, and so are his teeth. He gets mushers wax massaged into his paws to protect them. Once a week he gets his ears cleaned. Once a month he gets his nails clipped.

Favorite Toys: Squeaky toys Cat toys (that smell like catnip) Balls!

Training

Barrett was trained by an organization called NEADS which trains many types of assistance dogs. When Barrett was two months old he started his training which took place in a prison and was done by prison inmates, who were trained by professional trainers. He lived in the prison during the week, and then on weekends he was furloughed to a weekend puppy raiser who socialized him. When he was 18 months old, he was matched and has now been with his partner for almost 6 months.
